## How Nitrogen AI works

Nitrogen AI structures your project work into a guided flow — from initial description through evidence upload, module selection, and final document generation.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create a project">
    Describe your sustainable development project in plain language. Nitrogen AI captures the key details through a guided conversational intake.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Upload evidence">
    Add supporting documents, reports, or data. The platform uses retrieval-augmented generation (RAG) to ground all outputs in your materials.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Run assessments">
    Select from a catalog of modules: investment memos, LCOE models, carbon emission calculations, due diligence checklists, and more.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Export deliverables">
    Download polished, citation-backed documents in DOCX or XLSX — ready for investors, donors, or internal review.
  </Step>
</Steps>
