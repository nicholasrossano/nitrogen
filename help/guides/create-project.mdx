---
title: 'Create a project in Nitrogen AI'
description: 'Create a Nitrogen AI project by describing it in chat — no form, no fixed intake schema. Nitrogen AI infers what it can and asks for the rest conversationally.'
---

Creating a project is just describing it. There's no form and no required set of fields beyond a title — Nitrogen AI infers geography, sector, and other details directly from your description, and asks conversationally for anything important it's missing.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Start a new chat">
    From your dashboard, start a new chat and describe your project in plain language. This creates the project record immediately.

    ```text
    "A community-scale biogas digester program for 40 dairy farms in western Kenya,
    aimed at replacing charcoal cooking fuel and generating carbon credits."
    ```

    ```text
    "A district water utility in Malawi upgrading 12 boreholes to solar-powered
    pumping, serving roughly 18,000 people."
    ```

    <Tip>
      The more specific your description — technology, geography, who it serves — the better Nitrogen AI's project plan and assessment recommendations will be. But there's no minimum: you can start vague and refine as you go.
    </Tip>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Answer any follow-ups">
    If something important is missing or ambiguous, Nitrogen AI asks conversationally rather than blocking you with a form. Answer what you know; leave the rest for later.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  Every field — title, geography, sector, goal, budget range, timeline — can be edited at any time from the project, no matter how or when it was originally set.
</Note>
