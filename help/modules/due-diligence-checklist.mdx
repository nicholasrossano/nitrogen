The Due Diligence Checklist module gives you a systematic way to evaluate a project before committing funds or resources. Rather than starting from a blank spreadsheet, you get a checklist that is already calibrated to your project type, geography, and stage — with specific questions under each category, risk ratings for each item, and a prioritised list of what to tackle first.

## What you get

The output is an `.xlsx` spreadsheet with one sheet per checklist category. Each row is a specific checklist item with:

- The item description and key questions to investigate
- A risk rating if the item is not addressed (low / medium / high)
- Space to record your findings and status

The workbook also includes a summary tab with the overall risk rating, top priority items, and recommended next steps.

## Default categories

Nitrogen AI structures the checklist around six default categories, then adapts them to your project context during the alignment step.

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Technical Feasibility" icon="gears">

  </Card>
  <Card title="Financial Viability" icon="chart-line">
    Revenue model, cost structure, funding requirements, and financial projections.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Organizational Capacity" icon="users">
    Team experience, governance structure, partnerships, and operational readiness.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Regulatory & Legal" icon="shield">
    Required permits, compliance status, legal structure, and contractual arrangements.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Social & Environmental Impact" icon="leaf">
    Beneficiary identification, community engagement, environmental safeguards, and impact measurement.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Sustainability & Exit" icon="arrow-right">
    Path to financial sustainability, scale-up potential, exit or transition strategy, and capacity building.
  </Card>

<Note>
These are starting points. The alignment step lets you add, remove, rename, or reprioritise categories to match your specific due diligence requirements.
</Note>

## How it works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Provide project details">
    Tell Nitrogen AI about the project: title, type, geography, stage, and any specific areas of concern. The module pre-fills what it can from your workspace.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review the alignment">
    Nitrogen AI generates a proposed checklist structure showing each category, its description, and 3–5 focus areas tailored to your project type and context. You can request changes in plain language — for example, "add more focus on grid connection risks" or "remove the sustainability section for this early-stage pilot".
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm and generate">
    Once you approve the structure, Nitrogen AI produces the full checklist with specific, actionable items and investigative questions for each focus area.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Export to XLSX">
    Download the checklist as an `.xlsx` workbook and use it to track your review progress.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Inputs

<ParamField path="body.project_title" type="string">
  The name of the project being evaluated.

  *Example: Solar Mini-Grid Pilot in Northern Kenya*
</ParamField>

<ParamField path="body.project_type" type="string">
  The sector or technology type. Used to calibrate which risks and questions are most relevant.

  Options: `Energy Access`, `Clean Cooking`, `Agriculture`, `Water & Sanitation`, `Health`, `Other`
</ParamField>

<ParamField path="body.geography" type="string">
  Country or region where the project operates. Affects regulatory and community engagement questions.

  *Example: Kenya, Northern Region*
</ParamField>

<ParamField path="body.project_stage" type="string">
  The current development stage. A concept-stage project needs different questions than an operational one.

  Options: `Concept/Idea`, `Feasibility Study`, `Pilot`, `Scale-up`, `Operational`
</ParamField>

<ParamField path="body.key_concerns" type="string">
  Any specific areas you want the checklist to focus on or dig deeper into.

  *Example: Regulatory compliance for grid connection, community land rights*
</ParamField>

## Alignment options

<ParamField path="body.detail_level" type="string" default="standard">
  How detailed each checklist item is. Options: `brief` (concise items), `standard` (moderate detail), `detailed` (comprehensive questions for each item).
</ParamField>

<ParamField path="body.risk_focus" type="string" default="balanced">
  Whether the checklist leans toward surfacing risks or identifying opportunities.

  Options: `opportunity-focused`, `balanced`, `risk-focused`
</ParamField>

## Export format

| Format | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| XLSX | Excel workbook with categorised checklist items, risk ratings, investigation questions, priority items, and next steps. |
