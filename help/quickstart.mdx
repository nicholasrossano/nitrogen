This guide walks you through the complete Nitrogen AI workflow from first sign-up to your first exported deliverable. You'll create a project, upload supporting evidence, select a module, confirm the outline, generate your output, and export it — all in one session.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Sign up for Nitrogen AI">
    Go to [app.the-nitrogen.ai](https://app.the-nitrogen.ai) and create your account using your email address.

    After confirming your email, you'll land on the home screen. New accounts start on the **trial tier**, which lets you explore the platform before committing to a subscription or adding your own API key.

    <Tip>
      If your organization already has a Nitrogen AI account, ask your admin to invite you directly rather than signing up separately. This keeps your projects under a shared workspace.
    </Tip>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Create a project">
    From the home screen, click **New project**.

    Describe your project in the chat using plain language — where it's located, what it aims to do, who it's for, and any relevant technical or financial details you already have. You don't need everything ready upfront.

    ```text
    Example: "We're developing a 5 MW solar-plus-storage mini-grid in rural Nigeria to 
    serve 3,000 households and small businesses currently without reliable grid access. 
    We're seeking development finance and need an investment memo and LCOE analysis."
    ```

    Nitrogen AI uses your description to structure the project record and to recommend the most relevant modules for your project type.

    <Tip>
      Be as specific as you can about geography, technology type, scale, and the audience for your deliverables. The more context you give, the more targeted the module recommendations will be.
    </Tip>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Upload evidence documents">
    Go to the **Evidence** tab within your project and upload your supporting materials.

    **Supported formats:** PDF, DOCX, XLSX — up to 50 MB per file.

    Evidence can include feasibility studies, site reports, financial models, prior assessments, letters of support, grant agreements, or any other relevant materials. Nitrogen AI uses these documents to ground every assessment and deliverable in your actual project context, with traceable citations in each output.

    <Note>
      You can upload evidence at any point — before or after selecting modules. Adding evidence before running an assessment gives the AI more material to work with and generally produces more accurate, specific outputs.
    </Note>

    <Warning>
      Do not upload documents containing personally identifiable information (PII) that is not relevant to the project assessment. Uploaded evidence is used to generate deliverables and may be referenced in citations.
    </Warning>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Select modules">
    Go to the **Modules** tab. Nitrogen AI automatically surfaces modules relevant to your project based on your description.

    Review the recommended modules and select the ones you want to run. Common starting points include:

    - **Investment memo** — a structured, investor-ready document covering project rationale, financials, risk, and impact
    - **Due diligence checklist** — a gap analysis against standard criteria used by development finance institutions
    - **LCOE model** — levelized cost of electricity calculation for renewable energy projects
    - **Carbon model** — estimated greenhouse gas emission reductions with methodology grounding

    You can run multiple modules on the same project. Each module draws on the same uploaded evidence and project details.

    <Tip>
      If you're unsure where to start, run the **Investment memo** module first. It produces a comprehensive overview that often informs which other modules to run next.
    </Tip>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Review and confirm the outline">
    Before Nitrogen AI begins generating the full deliverable, it presents a structured outline for your review — this is the **alignment step**.

    The outline shows you the sections and key points the deliverable will cover, based on your project description and uploaded evidence. Review it carefully and confirm that it reflects your goals.

    If something is missing or misaligned, you can provide feedback in the chat before confirming. Nitrogen AI will adjust the outline accordingly.

    <Info>
      Taking a moment to review the outline is the most effective way to shape the final output before generation runs. It's much easier to redirect the deliverable at this stage than to edit a completed document.
    </Info>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Generate the deliverable">
    Once you confirm the outline, click **Generate** to run the assessment.

    Nitrogen AI processes your project information and uploaded evidence to produce the deliverable. Generation typically takes between a few seconds and a few minutes depending on the module and the volume of evidence.

    Each output includes citations linking back to specific passages in your uploaded documents, so you can verify the reasoning behind any claim.

    <Info>
      If you haven't uploaded evidence yet, Nitrogen AI will still generate an output — but the content will draw on general precedent rather than your specific materials. Upload evidence first for the most accurate and defensible results.
    </Info>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Export to DOCX">
    Once generation is complete, click **Export** to download the deliverable.

    - Text-based deliverables — investment memos, assessments, checklists — export as **DOCX**
    - Financial models and data outputs export as **XLSX**

    The exported file is formatted and ready to share with investors, donors, or your internal team. No additional formatting is required.
  </Step>
</Steps>
