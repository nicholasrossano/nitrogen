The **project plan** is the central planning artifact for a project. Nitrogen AI generates it from your project description and organizes the work into **pillars** — thematic groupings — each containing concrete **items** you can track, research, and act on.

## Generating a plan

Nitrogen AI can generate a project plan once your project description has enough detail — at minimum, an identifiable geography and technology or project type. Ask for it directly in chat, or Nitrogen AI will offer to generate one automatically once enough context is available.

Generating a plan again after one exists refreshes it, incorporating any new evidence or project details since the last generation.

## Pillars and items

Each pillar groups related work into a named theme (for example, "Technical Feasibility" or "Financing & Structuring"). Every item inside a pillar has:

  <Card title="Item type" icon="tag">
    `deliverable` — a general work product to track manually, or `assessment` — something you can launch directly as one of Nitrogen AI's built-in assessments.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Classification" icon="flag">
    Whether the item is `required`, `optional`, or `unknown` for the project to move forward.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Status" icon="circle-check">
    `not_started`, `in_progress`, or `complete`. You can update this manually at any time.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Rationale" icon="comment">
    A short explanation of why this item matters for your specific project.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Deep dives

For any plan item, you can request a **deep dive** — a targeted research pass that produces a structured breakdown (what the item is, its component elements, dependencies, and cited sources) without leaving the plan. Deep-dive results are cached per item, so revisiting one you've already researched returns instantly instead of re-running research.

## Editing your plan

You're not limited to what Nitrogen AI generates. You can:

- **Add an item** to any pillar for something the AI didn't surface
- **Update an item's status** as you make progress
- **Remove an item** that isn't relevant to your project

## How the plan relates to assessments

Items with `item_type: assessment` map to one of Nitrogen AI's registered assessments (see [Assessments](/concepts/assessments)) — launching that item opens the corresponding assessment workspace. `deliverable` items are broader work products (partnership agreements, permits, financial close, and similar) that aren't produced by an assessment — you track their status manually as you complete the underlying work elsewhere.

<Tip>
  Use the project plan as your single source of truth for "what's left to do" on a project, and launch assessments directly from the items that need one.
</Tip>
