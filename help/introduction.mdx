Nitrogen AI is an AI-powered collaborative platform built for sustainable development project teams. It brings together guided project intake, evidence management, and AI-generated deliverables in one place — so you can move from initial concept to investor-ready outputs with evidence-backed reasoning at every step. Whether you're evaluating a solar project in a frontier market or preparing a grant application for a climate resilience program, Nitrogen AI gives your team expert-level analytical tools without the manual overhead.

## Who Nitrogen AI is for

Nitrogen AI is built for the people who design, fund, and evaluate sustainable development projects:

- **Development project teams** — manage project documentation, run assessments, and produce deliverables for investors and donors
- **NGOs and implementing organizations** — analyze projects across energy access, climate adaptation, and social development at scale
- **Investors and development finance institutions** — evaluate project viability, financial returns, and impact with consistent, traceable outputs
- **Sustainability consultants** — produce client-ready memos, checklists, and reports in a fraction of the time

## How it works

Nitrogen AI organizes your work into a clear, repeatable flow:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create a project">
    Describe your project in the chat. Nitrogen AI captures the key details through a conversational intake and structures them for use across all modules.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Upload evidence">
    Add PDFs, DOCX files, spreadsheets, feasibility studies, and any other supporting materials. The platform uses these documents to ground every assessment and deliverable in your actual project context.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select modules">
    Choose from a catalog of assessment and document-generation tools. Nitrogen AI recommends relevant modules based on your project description and type.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review and confirm the outline">
    Before generation begins, Nitrogen AI presents a structured outline of the deliverable for your review. Confirm or adjust it to ensure the output is aligned with your goals.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Generate deliverables">
    Run your selected modules and receive AI-generated outputs. Each deliverable includes citations linking back to specific passages in your uploaded documents.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Export">
    Download your deliverables as DOCX or XLSX files, ready to share with investors, donors, or your internal team.
  </Step>
</Steps>
