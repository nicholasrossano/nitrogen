Nitrogen AI is a chat-first platform for teams planning and evaluating sustainable development projects. Describe a project in plain language and Nitrogen AI builds a structured **project plan**, recommends the **assessments** you need to run, and tracks an ongoing **project status** — all grounded in the evidence you upload, with every AI claim traceable back to a source.

## Who Nitrogen AI is for

- **Development project teams** — plan an initiative, track workstreams, and produce assessment outputs for investors and donors
- **NGOs and implementing organizations** — analyze projects across energy access, climate adaptation, and social development at scale
- **Investors and development finance institutions** — evaluate project viability, risk, and economics with consistent, traceable outputs
- **Sustainability consultants** — produce client-ready plans, risk registers, and models in a fraction of the time

## How it works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Describe your project">
    Start a chat and describe your project in plain language — geography, technology, scale, and goals. Nitrogen AI captures this as a project record.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Upload evidence">
    Add supporting documents — feasibility studies, technical reports, financial models. The platform indexes them for retrieval so every output cites your actual materials.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Generate a project plan">
    Nitrogen AI produces a plan organized into pillars, each with concrete work items — some are documents or models to produce (assessments), others are general deliverables to track.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Run assessments">
    Launch any assessment from the plan. Assessments walk through a setup → build → output flow in a dedicated workspace, producing a DOCX or XLSX export.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Track project status">
    Nitrogen AI continuously assesses project status across a set of editable categories, so you always know what's healthy and what needs attention.
  </Step>
</Steps>
