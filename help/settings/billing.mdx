Nitrogen AI uses a tiered billing model so you can start for free and scale as your work grows. Every account starts on the trial tier. You can upgrade to a paid plan through Stripe or bring your own OpenAI API key to unlock unlimited AI access using your own credits.

## Billing tiers

<CardGroup cols={3}>
  <Card title="Trial" icon="flask">
    The default tier for new accounts. You get limited AI access to explore the platform before committing to a plan.
  </Card>
  <Card title="BYOK" icon="key">
    Bring your own OpenAI API key. AI generation uses your own OpenAI credits with no usage cap from Nitrogen AI. See [API Keys](/settings/api-keys) to set this up.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Paid subscription" icon="credit-card">
    Managed AI access billed through Stripe. Nitrogen AI handles the OpenAI costs on your behalf, up to your plan's monthly limit.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Subscribing to a paid plan

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open billing settings">
    Go to **Settings → Billing**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose a plan">
    Review the available plans and click **Subscribe** on the one that fits your needs.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete checkout">
    You'll be redirected to a Stripe-hosted checkout page. Enter your payment details and confirm. Once payment succeeds, your account is upgraded immediately.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  You need an active Nitrogen AI account before subscribing. If you haven't signed up yet, go to [the-nitrogen.ai](https://the-nitrogen.ai) to create one.
</Note>

## Managing your subscription

To update your payment method, view invoices, or cancel your plan, go to **Settings → Billing → Manage subscription**. This opens the Stripe Customer Portal, where you have full control over your billing details.

<Tip>
  If you cancel, your plan stays active until the end of the current billing period. You won't be charged again after that.
</Tip>

## Checking your usage

Your current usage is always available in **Settings → Billing**. The billing status shows:

| Field | Description |
|---|---|
| `allowed` | Whether AI features are currently available on your account |
| `tier` | Your current billing tier (`trial`, `byok`, `starter`, `pro`) |
| `used_usd` | AI spend so far in the current billing period (USD) |
| `limit_usd` | The monthly spend cap for your plan |

## Usage limits and blocked access

When you reach your plan's monthly usage limit, AI features are blocked until the next billing cycle or until you take one of these actions:

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Upgrade your plan" icon="arrow-up" href="/settings/billing">
    Move to a higher tier with a larger monthly limit via **Settings → Billing**.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Add a BYOK key" icon="key" href="/settings/api-keys">
    Connect your own OpenAI API key to bypass Nitrogen AI usage limits entirely.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

<Warning>
  While your account is blocked, you can still view existing projects, deliverables, and evidence — only AI generation is paused. No data is lost.
</Warning>

## Access codes

If you received an access code from the Nitrogen AI team, you can redeem it in **Settings → Billing** to extend your trial access. Each code can only be redeemed once per account.
