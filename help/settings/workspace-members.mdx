Workspace membership is managed from **Settings → Team**, separately from sharing an individual project. This page covers inviting and managing members of your company workspace.

## Invite a teammate

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Settings → Team">
    From your account settings, go to the **Team** section.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter their email address">
    Add the teammate's email. If they don't have a Nitrogen AI account yet, an invitation is created and redeemed automatically the first time they sign in.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm">
    They're added as a workspace member (or, if it's the very first member added to a new company workspace, as its owner).
  </Step>
</Steps>

## What workspace membership grants

Workspace membership is about **shared workspace resources**, not automatic project access:

- Access to workspace-level [knowledge banks](/concepts/workspaces#knowledge-banks)
- Ability to upload workspace-level materials
- Visibility into other workspace members for collaboration

<Warning>
  Workspace membership does **not** automatically give a member access to every project in the workspace. Each project still needs its own share — see [Share and collaborate](/guides/share-and-collaborate). This keeps individual projects private by default, even within a shared team workspace.
</Warning>

## Roles

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Owner" icon="crown">
    Can add and remove members and administer workspace-level resources. The first member of a company workspace becomes its owner.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Member" icon="user-group">
    Can use workspace-level resources but cannot manage membership.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Remove a member or cancel an invitation

From **Settings → Team**, find the member or pending invitation in the list and remove it. Removing a member revokes their access to workspace-level resources immediately; it does not affect any individual projects they were separately given access to via project sharing.
